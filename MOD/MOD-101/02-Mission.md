# 2. Mission

Transform an exciting idea into an intentional story.

Everything within Story Discovery supports this single objective.

The Module does not attempt to create a plot, design characters in detail, construct scenes, or build an outline. Those responsibilities belong to later Modules after the story's purpose has been clearly understood.

Instead, Story Discovery answers the foundational questions that every successful novel must ultimately resolve:

- What is this story fundamentally about?
- Why does the Author want to tell it?
- What emotional experience should readers have?
- What makes this story worth developing instead of another idea?
- What promises are being made to the reader?
- What questions should guide every future design decision?

When these questions have been answered with confidence, Story Discovery has achieved its mission.
