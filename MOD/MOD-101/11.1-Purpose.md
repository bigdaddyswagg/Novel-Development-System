# 11.1 Purpose

Validation ensures that Story Discovery has achieved its intended transformation before the project advances to subsequent Modules.

The objective of validation is not to determine whether the story is "good."

Creative merit remains a matter of artistic judgment.

Instead, validation determines whether the Module has successfully fulfilled its responsibility within the Novel Development System by transforming inspiration into an intentional and sufficiently understood Story Vision.

Validation protects downstream development from being built upon an unstable creative foundation.
