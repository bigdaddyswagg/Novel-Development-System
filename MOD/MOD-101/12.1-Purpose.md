# 12.1 Purpose

Story Discovery assumes that creative development is inherently exploratory and that periods of uncertainty, confusion, and redirection are natural components of the process.

Accordingly, not every interruption represents failure.

This section distinguishes between productive creative uncertainty and conditions that prevent the Module from achieving its intended transformation.

The objective is not to eliminate difficulty.

The objective is to recognize when intervention is necessary and provide a structured path back to productive discovery.
