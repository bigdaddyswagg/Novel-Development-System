# 15.5 Gold Master Relationship

MOD-101 serves as the canonical implementation of the MOD-000 Module Standard.

Future operational Modules shall use MOD-101 as the reference implementation for:

- structural organization;
- engineering conventions;
- metadata;
- validation;
- quality assurance;
- traceability;
- repository formatting.

Architectural improvements to the operational module pattern should be evaluated against MOD-101 before being adopted across the repository.
