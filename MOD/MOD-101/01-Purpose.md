# 1. Purpose

Story Discovery is the foundational operational Module of the Novel Development System (NDS).

Its purpose is to transform an exciting but incomplete story idea into an intentional, validated Story Vision that serves as the creative foundation for every subsequent stage of novel development.

Story Discovery is intentionally positioned as the Author's first experience of the Novel Development System. Rather than immediately solving creative problems, it establishes a collaborative discovery process that allows the Author and AI to investigate the story together before making significant design decisions.

The Module recognizes that inspiration is often incomplete but rarely accidental. A compelling image, a single scene, an intriguing character, an emotional question, or a fleeting "what if?" may all contain the beginnings of an exceptional novel. Story Discovery exists to uncover the larger story hidden within those fragments.

By the conclusion of this Module, the Author should possess not merely an interesting idea, but a validated understanding of what story they intend to tell, why it matters, what promises it makes to readers, and why it deserves the substantial investment required to develop it into a novel.

The resulting Story Vision becomes the governing creative intent for every downstream Module within the Novel Development System.
