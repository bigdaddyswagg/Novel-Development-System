# 10.5 Iterative Discovery

Story Discovery is intentionally iterative.

Authors should expect to revisit earlier conversations as new understanding emerges.

An idea that appears insignificant during one session may later become the foundation of the entire novel.

Likewise, concepts that initially seem essential may gradually disappear as the story reveals a stronger direction.

This behavior is not inefficiency.

It is evidence that genuine discovery is occurring.

## Revisiting Earlier Conclusions

No conclusion reached during Story Discovery should be considered permanent.

Whenever new information contradicts an earlier assumption, the Author and AI should re-evaluate that assumption without hesitation.

Creative progress depends upon improving understanding rather than defending previous decisions.

## Productive Uncertainty

Not every question should be answered immediately.

Some questions become more valuable when allowed to remain unresolved.

Maintaining productive uncertainty encourages additional exploration and prevents the story from becoming constrained by premature certainty.

The AI should distinguish between uncertainty that reflects incomplete understanding and uncertainty that stimulates creative discovery.

The first should eventually be resolved.

The second should often be preserved until later Modules.

## Discovery Through Conversation

Conversation is the primary operating mechanism of Story Discovery.

Meaningful insights frequently emerge while discussing subjects that initially appear unrelated.

The AI should therefore treat every conversation as an opportunity for discovery rather than as a sequence of isolated questions and answers.

Unexpected connections are often among the most valuable outcomes of the Module.

## Creative Momentum

Momentum should be protected whenever possible.

When the Author is actively discovering new aspects of the story, administrative tasks, rigid workflows, and unnecessary documentation should remain secondary.

The purpose of the methodology is to support creativity.

It must never become an obstacle to it.
