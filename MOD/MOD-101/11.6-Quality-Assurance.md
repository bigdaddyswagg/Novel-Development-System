# 11.6 Quality Assurance

Repository quality extends beyond successful creative discovery.

Every implementation of Story Discovery should also satisfy the following engineering expectations.

The Module shall:

- preserve Author ownership throughout the process;
- encourage exploration before commitment;
- distinguish discovery from design;
- document significant creative decisions;
- preserve unresolved questions for future Modules;
- produce a validated Story Vision;
- support repeatable application across projects;
- remain consistent with the governing architecture of the Novel Development System.

# 11.7 Quality Characteristics

A repository-quality implementation of Story Discovery demonstrates:

- clarity;
- consistency;
- repeatability;
- adaptability;
- traceability;
- author-centric collaboration;
- architectural compliance.

These qualities establish Story Discovery as a dependable operational Module rather than a collection of creative exercises.
