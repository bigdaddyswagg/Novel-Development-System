# 11.3 Validation Responsibilities

Validation is a collaborative activity.

## Author Responsibilities

The Author is responsible for evaluating:

- personal enthusiasm;
- confidence in the Story Vision;
- creative authenticity;
- willingness to continue development;
- alignment between the story and the original creative intent.

The Author alone determines whether the story represents the novel they genuinely wish to write.

## AI Responsibilities

The AI is responsible for evaluating:

- internal consistency;
- unresolved contradictions;
- incomplete assumptions;
- opportunities for clarification;
- readiness for downstream development.

The AI provides analysis and recommendations.

It does not approve or reject the story.
