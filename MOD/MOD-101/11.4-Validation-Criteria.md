# 11.4 Validation Criteria

Story Discovery should demonstrate the following characteristics before completion.

## Story Identity

The story possesses a recognizable identity independent of its original inspiration.

## Creative Direction

The Author understands the primary direction of the novel.

## Reader Promise

The intended reader experience has been articulated.

## Narrative Sustainability

The concept appears capable of supporting the intended scope of the project.

## Author Commitment

The Author remains enthusiastic about developing the story.

## Development Readiness

Future Modules can proceed without repeatedly rediscovering the story's purpose.
