# 12.5 Escalation Guidelines

When repeated recovery attempts fail to restore productive discovery, the Author should consider one or more of the following actions:

- Revisit the original source of inspiration.
- Reduce the scope of the concept.
- Explore alternative premises.
- Challenge core assumptions.
- Introduce new research or reference material.
- Temporarily suspend the project and return after an appropriate period of reflection.

The objective is not to force progress.

The objective is to restore meaningful creative momentum.
