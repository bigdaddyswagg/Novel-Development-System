# 11.5 Validation Outcomes

Validation may produce one of three outcomes.

## Ready to Proceed

The Story Vision demonstrates sufficient clarity.

Development advances to the next Module.

## Continue Discovery

Additional exploration is recommended.

Story Discovery continues until the remaining uncertainty has been reduced to an acceptable level.

## Reconsider Direction

Validation reveals that the current concept no longer represents the story the Author wishes to tell.

This outcome should not be viewed as failure.

Discovering that an idea should evolve, or even be abandoned, before significant development begins represents successful application of Story Discovery.
