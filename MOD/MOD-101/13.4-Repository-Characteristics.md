# 13.4 Repository Characteristics

A repository-quality implementation of Story Discovery exhibits the following characteristics.

## Consistency

The Module follows the conventions established by MOD-000 and demonstrated by the MOD-101 Gold Master.

## Traceability

Major decisions, outputs, and relationships can be traced to governing artifacts and downstream consumers.

## Maintainability

Engineering improvements can be incorporated without redesigning the methodology.

## Extensibility

Future releases may expand engineering capabilities while preserving the underlying creative process.

## Interoperability

The Story Vision produced by this Module serves as a stable input for subsequent NDS Modules without requiring reinterpretation.
