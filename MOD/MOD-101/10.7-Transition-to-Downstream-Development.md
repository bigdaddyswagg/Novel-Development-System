# 10.7 Transition to Downstream Development

Completion of Story Discovery marks the end of exploratory development and the beginning of intentional design.

Responsibility shifts from discovering the story to systematically developing it.

Subsequent Modules inherit the Story Vision as their governing creative reference.

Every major design decision should remain consistent with the intent established during Story Discovery unless the Author deliberately revises that intent based upon new evidence or meaningful creative discovery.

The Story Vision therefore serves both as a foundation and as a point of reference throughout the remainder of the Novel Development System.

Its purpose is not to restrict creativity.

Its purpose is to preserve intentionality.

## Module Completion Statement

Story Discovery is complete when inspiration has been transformed into intentional direction.

The Author understands the story well enough to begin designing it with confidence while remaining open to the discoveries that accompany every successful novel.

The Module has fulfilled its purpose when future development proceeds from understanding rather than speculation.

_End of Operational Methodology_
